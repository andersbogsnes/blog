About Me

I'm a relatively recent convert to Python having used it for around 2-3 years now. I've been working in the insurance industry for about 5 years now, mainly involved in customer analytics and sales analytics. I started as an Excel power user but I grew quickly frustrated when working with large amounts of data. Tried all sorts of hacks to speed things up, but it just seemed very cumbersome.

There had to be a better way!

That's when I found Python.

Python lets me fix all sorts of problems I didn't even know I had! From generating reports to creating analyses and forecasts to automating the boring stuff -- Python is the answer
